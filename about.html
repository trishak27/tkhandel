About Me

I am a professional photographer based in New York City. I specialize in portrait and fashion photography and have been working in the industry for over 10 years. I have a passion for capturing beauty and creating stunning visuals that tell a story.

My work has been featured in top fashion magazines and websites such as Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Elle. I have also worked with major fashion brands and celebrities.

When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy traveling and exploring new places. I believe that experiencing new cultures and meeting new people is essential for my creativity and inspiration.
